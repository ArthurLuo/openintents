Calendar Picker features:
=========================================

Uses:
* Event selection
* Date selection
* Event visualization

Interaction:
* D-pad navigation between dates
* D-pad center or pointer tap activates a date
* Horizontal fling increments/decrements month
* Vertical fling scrolls quickly through months
* Dragging on timeline changes the date
* Longpressable dates
* Clicking on a date with events brings up an event list
